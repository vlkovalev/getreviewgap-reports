The whole build in five moves

Keep the production simple: storyboard first, generate only the frames you need, and let PowerPoint handle the final choreography.

01

Storyboard the frames

Decide the title frame, product frame, text side, menu details, and every transition target before generating assets.

02

Generate the visual world

Create branded glass spheres, product backgrounds, and clean 16:9 compositions in an image model.

03

Add brand marks

Use the logo as a reference and ask AI to place small, realistic marks only on selected objects.

04

Create transition video

Set the first generated frame as the start image and the next product frame as the end image.

05

Animate in PowerPoint

Place video behind text, auto-play it, then delay text entrance until the video lands on the product composition.
